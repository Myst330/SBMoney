Tier 3

Tier 3, end game, the best, those that have progressed through the game more than mid-game.

Ghosts

Ghosts are incredibly strong creatures that can be found within the Dwarven Mines.

Upon entering the area they are located, "The Mist," a message will appear on your screen, warning of the dangers.

These creatures cannot deal long ranged attacks, but their aura will harm you if you are within a certain distance of them.

Entering the dwarven mines should be very easy, considering you're an endgame player.

From destroying this creatures, you can get a variety of drops, and although most of them are quite rare, they are not as rare as tier 2's method.

Drops could include 1,000,000 coins, which has a drop rate of .01%, sorrow at 0.12%, and a few other pretty common items.

This money making method is good because a sorrow will go for around 310,000 coins on the auction house.

If you're wondering, sorrow is used to craft Sorrow Armor, which ironically, can raise your chances of getting more sorrow.

Sorrow armor increases a stat called Magic Find, which increases the chances of getting rare drops.

An item with a drop rate of 1% will be doubled if your magic find is 100, making it 2%
