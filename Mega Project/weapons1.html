Tier 1 Utility

As said on the main page, tier 1 does not exactly require much equipment, since it's what you do to get the equipment.

But even though that is true, there are still some things I recommend.

Firstly, the "Rookie Hoe."

The Rookie Hoe is obtainable by buying it from the farm merchant for about 20 coins. -->

The reason I recommend this tool is because it has a special ability/bonus (hover to view).

Each time you break a crop with this tool, it has a 50% chance to drop seeds.

This tool is good because seeds, like wheat and other crops, can be sold for money. Even though seeds have dropped in price, and although they barely sell for any money, it's better than nothing.


The next on my list is the "Farm Suit" set.

The farm suit is one of the most basic armor sets. It can be unlocked at Wheat collection 3, meaning you have to collect 250 wheat, which, for context, is not that much.

The reason I recommend this armor set- not tool- is because it boosts your speed on farm islands. Speed is crucial when farming, because it allows you to collect crops quicker, ultimately leading to faster money.

While there are obviously other ways to boost speed, this way is most likely the most basic.
