Tier 1

A Tier 1 player is an absolute beginner, a person that is playing the game for their first time.

For Tier 1, I recommend farming wheat to sell to the farming merchant, located in the Hub.

Farming wheat is quite efficient, because there is already a practically infinite amount of wheat fairly close to the middle of the Hub.

Farming wheat does not need any particular tools or gear, thus making it a viable option for newer players.

But farming wheat manually isn't the only way of farming, you can also install minions!

If you aren't familiar with Skyblock minions, this is how they work:


Once farming enough wheat, your wheat collection will level up. A collection is basically how much you have collected something. As your collection level progresses, you will be able to craft/create more efficient, stronger tools.

One of the most basic recipes you unlock is a minion recipe, unlocked at wheat I, meaning you must collect 50 wheat. Minions are usually crafted with materials matching their use. A minion farming wheat can be crafted by using wheat, a minion farming potatoes can be crafted with potatoes, so on and so forth.

Minions, as mentioned earlier, do your work for you, making it easier to earn money.
