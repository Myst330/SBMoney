Tier 1 Rates

After performing 3 hour long tests, I have returned with these results.

A tool with the telekinesis enchantment would make...

15,330 coins/hour

With bonuses: 20,430/hour

Wheat collected: ~9000

Seeds collected: ~12660


A rookie hoe with the harvest and telekinesis enchantments would make...

29,900 coins/hour

With bonuses: 31,100/hour

Wheat collected: ~22,300

Seeds collected: ~30,800


As expected, the rookie hoe can collect more per hour than a normal tool (with telekinesis)

Although, there is one thing that makes the rookie hoe stand out a bit more, one thing that lets it take the lead

The "Harvest" enchantment.

The harvest enchantment is quite simple. It gives you more crops.

Along with the Rookie Hoe ability, which gives a 50% chance to drop seeds, we get more crops and seeds from the harvest enchantment.

I would show the results from farming with the farm suit, but it's difficult to do 3, hour long tests consecutively. It makes you go faster, and that's all I know.
