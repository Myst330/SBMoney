Tier 2 Utility

Due to zealots having 13,000 health points, you're going to have to do enough, preferably 13,000, damage if you want to use this method efficiently.

The weapon I recommend for this method is the Aspect of the Dragons

The Aspect of the Dragons, AOTD, is a weapon that does great damage.

It can be obtained in two ways; acquiring it from the dragon fight, which takes place in the Dragon's Nest, or buying it from the auction house.

As of now, AOTDs cost about 600,000 coins.

Which can be earned with about 20 hours of farming wheat! Just kidding, but that would be accurate.


The armor I would recommend for this method would be Ender Armor.

Ender Armor is recommended because of it's bonus.

When inside the End Dimension, the armor's stats are doubled. Reforges, enchantments, everything.

Now I'm going to be honest, using this armor outside of the End Dimension is quite useless.
