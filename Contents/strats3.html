Strategy 1. The only Strategy here...

SWORD SWAPPING

An insanely overpowered, but unintended, technique.

It's a bit difficult to explain...

Basically, you fire something, and switch to another weapon before it hits, therefore gaining it's new attack stat, dealing damage as if it was your most recent weapon.

Now as I said, the Giant's Sword wields big damage, but it can only hit one opponent at the time. The Soul Whip does small damage, but it can hit multiple enemies. See where I'm going with this?

By flaying your Soul Whip, you can hit multiple enemies, but before it hits the ghosts, if you switch to your giant's sword, the attack launched by your soul whip gains the giant's sword's stats, making it superpowered!!!!
